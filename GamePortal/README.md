This game portal can be used to play three games through one program.

The first game is the Number Guess Game, where the computer chooses a random number from 1 to 100. The objective is to find the number by guessing numbers until you reach the correct one, where the computer gives you hints on whether to go lower or higher after each guess. Your high score here depends on how many guesses it took you to reach the number within a round.

The second game is BlackJack, where you are taken to a seperate UI to play. In Blackjack, the objective is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Cards are face value, face cards are 10, and aces can be 1 or 11. You get two cards and can choose to hit (take another) or stand (stop). If you go over 21, you bust. After your turn is over, the dealer reveals their cards and hits until at least a hard 17. The higher total wins; a tie is a draw or push.

The third game is a Breaking Bad themed BuzzFeed Quiz. Answer all of the multiple choice questions and learn the Breaking Bad main character you are most similar to at the end. Your high score here depends on the amount of answers you choose that are correlated to your character, meaning that getting a higher score means you are more similar to the person you recieve.
